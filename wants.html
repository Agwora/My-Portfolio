What I Want.

I believe the best working relationships start with clarity. So here's mine — no ambiguity, no corporate filter. Just what I need to grow, what I want to build, and what I bring in return.

What I need (to be pushed)
  • A manager who challenges me — not one who just manages me.
  • Honest feedback. Even when it's uncomfortable. Especially then.
  • Problems that don't have obvious answers.
  • A team where "I don't know" is the start of a conversation, not the end of one.
  • Permission to fail fast, learn faster, and iterate openly.
What I want (in my career)
  • To move from "reporting what happened" to "predicting what's next."
  • To work where data empowers people — not just tracks them.
  • To earn the title Data Scientist through real impact, not time served.
  • To look back in 5 years knowing I grew faster than I expected.
  • To build systems that outlast any single role I hold.
